# Fun Small Talk — Brand Voice

## Tone
Warm, direct, honest. Real person who figured it out — not a guru, not a life coach.
Ali Abdaal-style: relatable, data-backed, no cringe motivation fluff.

## What We Sound Like
- "I used to dread walking into rooms where I didn't know anyone. Here's what changed."
- "Small talk gets a bad reputation. But it's the doorway to every real conversation you've ever had."
- "This isn't about being an extrovert. It's about having the tools when you need them."

## What We Never Sound Like
- "UNLOCK YOUR FULL POTENTIAL!!!"
- "Are you tired of being awkward? This ONE trick..."
- "I made $10K just from networking — here's how YOU can too"

## Key Messages
1. Social skills are learnable - like any skill
2. This was built for introverts and post-pandemic rusty people
3. Real frameworks, real practice, real results - not theory
4. The course is a system, not just videos

## Course Name
Fun Small Talk — always use full name on first mention, FST acceptable shorthand after

## Tagline
Stop Fearing The Awkward Silence
