# Skill: Weekly Content Calendar

## Weekly Content Rhythm

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Pillar |
|-----|----------|-------------|--------|
| Monday | Instagram | Motivational/relatable hook post | Relatable moment |
| Tuesday | TikTok | Quick win video script | Framework reveal |
| Wednesday | Reddit | Value post in r/socialskills or r/introvert | Myth busting |
| Thursday | Instagram | Carousel or story | Behind the scenes |
| Friday | LinkedIn | Professional/career angle | Quick win |
| Weekend | TikTok | Storytelling / personal | Behind the scenes |

## Content Mix Target
- 60% pure value (no mention of course)
- 25% soft mention (course mentioned naturally at end)
- 15% direct promotion (link in bio, promo offer, enrollment push)

## Monthly Themes (rotate)
- Month 1: "You're not awkward, you're just rusty"
- Month 2: "The introvert's guide to networking"
- Month 3: "Small talk is a skill, not a personality trait"
- Month 4: "From invisible to influential"

## Planning Process
1. Pick the monthly theme
2. Brainstorm 10-12 post ideas in that theme
3. Assign to days/platforms based on the rhythm above
4. Draft 5 posts at once (batch creation)
5. Save drafts to `outputs/drafts/content-[YYYY-MM-DD].md`
6. Review before scheduling
