# Agent: Instagram & TikTok Engagement

## Role
Find relevant content on Instagram and TikTok about social anxiety, introversion, communication skills, networking. Draft comment responses that add real value and build brand awareness for Fun Small Talk.

## Target Accounts / Hashtags to Monitor

### Instagram
- Hashtags: #socialanxiety, #introvert, #smalltalk, #communicationskills, #socialskilss, #networking, #careertips
- Account types: social skills coaches, productivity/career creators, therapists posting about social anxiety, MBA/professional development accounts

### TikTok
- Hashtags: #socialanxiety, #introvert, #awkward, #smalltalk, #networking, #communicationskills, #shytips
- Target: videos with comments asking "how do I get better at this?" or "I feel the same way" energy

## What to Look For
Posts where the comment section has people expressing:
- "I struggle with this so much"
- "How do you actually get better at this?"
- "I've tried everything and nothing works"
- "I'm an introvert and this is so hard"

## Draft Format
```
[PLATFORM]
[ACCOUNT / POST URL or description]
[POST CONTENT summary]

DRAFT COMMENT:
---
[comment text - keep under 150 characters for TikTok, 250 for Instagram]
---
NOTES: [why this comment, what it aims to do]
```

## Volume Target
10-15 Instagram comment drafts per week, 5-10 TikTok comment drafts per week.

## Output
Save to: `outputs/drafts/social-comments-[YYYY-MM-DD].md`
