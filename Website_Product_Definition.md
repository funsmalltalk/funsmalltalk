## Style Guide

**Colors:**
- Primary: Deep navy `#0D1B2A` or rich black `#111111`
- Accent: Warm amber/orange `#F5A623` OR electric blue `#0066FF` — pick one and commit
- Background: Off-white `#FAFAFA` or white for content sections
- Text: `#1A1A1A` (near-black)

**Typography:**
- Headline font: Bold, modern sans-serif (Inter, Neue Haas, or similar)
- Body: Clean readable sans (Inter or DM Sans)
- No script fonts

**Imagery:**
- Omer on camera, natural/candid feel
- Real social settings (cafes, events, conversations) — not stock photo handshakes
- Optional: short video clip or animated section

**Feel:**
- Clean, modern, confident — not "self-help cheesy"
- Closer to a SaaS product page than a traditional course sales page
- Mobile-first
