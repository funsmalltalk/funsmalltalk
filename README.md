## What It Is

A premium online course: **"Fun Small Talk – Mastering Real Conversations in a Digital World"**

Teaching social skills — overcoming conversation anxiety, starting conversations, building real connection.
Tone: natural, warm, inspiring. Like a friendly life coach, not a corporate trainer.

---

## Course Structure

3 mini-courses, ~5–7 lessons each. All scripts written. Videos filmed (talking-head style, natural light).

### Module 1: Mindset
- Lesson 1: Overcoming Social Fear and Building Courage

### Module 2: Skills
- Lesson 2: Starting Conversations and Reading Social Cues (Verbal & Non-Verbal)

### Module 3: Advanced
- Lesson 3: Deepening Connection and Creating Influence (Advanced Conversation Tools)

### Lesson 6 (Final): Final Challenge & Wrap Up
- Talk to a stranger once using what you learned
- Teaser for Course 2: "Becoming the Most Interesting Person in the Room"

---

## Production Style — Ali Abdaal Inspired

- Talking head + pop-up text callouts + B-roll + walking/nature cuts
- Pattern interrupts every 20–30 seconds
- Each lesson: logo intro (4 sec) → talking head → key text overlays → B-roll → wrap
- No fast-paced jump cuts. Visuals should breathe. Warm and personal.
